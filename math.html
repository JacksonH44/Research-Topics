Cryptography

Cryptography is the practice and study of techniques for secure communication. Computer era cryptography has basis in information theory, computational complexity, statistics, combinatorics, abstract algebra, and number theory, among other areas.

Machine Learning

Machine learning is the study of comptuer algorithms that can improve automatically through experience and by the use of data. Machine learning can be thought of as a subset of artificial intelligence with close connections to data mining, optimization, and statistics.

Computational Genomics

Computational genomics refers to the use of computational and statistics analysis to decipher biology from genome sequences and related data. Computational genomics can be thought of as a subset of bioinformatics and computational biology, but with more of a focus on whole genomes.
